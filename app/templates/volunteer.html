Role of volunteer 

                The role of a Crisis Responder is to bring texters from a “hot” moment to a cool calm,
                
                and to help them come up with a plan to stay healthy and safe.
                
                Our Volunteers are trained to do this using active listening and collaborative problem-solving by undergoing our
                regress Training Programmes.

                During an emergency , our volunteers are ready to implement those skills to save lives.
